Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

Privacy Commitment: This extension is built with a "Privacy-First" philosophy. We believe your gaming habits should remain yours alone. This extension does not track you, profile you, or transmit your data.

1. Data Collection & Handling

Tic Tac Toe Online: Classic XO Game is a client-side application.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Local Storage only: All game states (current board, score, and game mode) are processed locally within your browser's temporary memory. This data is not synced to the cloud and is cleared when the session ends.

2. Browser Permissions (Manifest V3)

We only request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:

  • sidePanel: This permission is used exclusively to allow users to play the game in Chrome's side panel, facilitating a better multitasking experience. It does not allow us to see your browsing activity.

3. AI Processing

The "Vs AI" mode utilizes a local Minimax Algorithm. All decision-making calculations are performed on your local machine's CPU. No data is sent to external servers for AI computation.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. This extension contains no third-party advertisements, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics).

5. Compliance

This extension complies with the Google Web Store Developer Terms of Service and the User Data Privacy Policy.
