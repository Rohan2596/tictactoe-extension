Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This Tic-Tac-Toe Chrome Extension has been designed with simplicity and user safety in mind. This page explains how your data is handled.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. All gameplay happens entirely within your browser and works offline.

Permissions

The extension requires no special permissions. It does not access your browsing history, personal files, or any online resources.

Offline Use

The extension is fully functional without an internet connection. No data is transmitted to any server.

Third Parties

No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third parties. The extension contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking scripts.

Updates

If this extension is updated in the future, and if any data-related functionality is introduced, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified via the Chrome Web Store.
